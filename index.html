X-Ray Vision: DL-Powered Chest Disease Diagnosis Platform

Built an end-to-end web platform that lets clinicians upload DICOM chest X-rays and instantly receive AI-powered diagnoses for 14 radiograph diseases. Trained a multi-label classification model on the NIH ChestX-ray14 dataset (112,000+ images), achieving an AUC of 0.767 with a lightweight ResNet-9 architecture. Integrated Grad-CAM to generate visual explanations — highlighting the exact image regions that drove each prediction, supporting interpretable and trustworthy clinical AI.
